Hadrian's Wall, or Vallum Aelium in Latin, was begun in AD 122 during the reign of Emperor Hadrian. Built to protect the kingdom of Britannia, it now lies entirely south of the Scottish border. The later Antonine Wall, started in AD 142, however lies entirely within modern day Scotland.
