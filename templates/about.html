About WeeQuiz

I started WeeQuiz through love for the irrepressible beauty of Scotland. I first travelled there as a kid and have never really stopped. WeeQuiz is dedicated to all those glens, lochs, munros and wee towns that I have visited or am yet to visit. Here's to you Scotland!

Scotland

For as long as I remember I have always loved Scotland and each journey (read pilgrimage) I make there only makes my feelings grow stronger. Although born and bred in Wales I have pretty much visited Scotland every single year of my life. I have climbed the northernmost (Ben Hope) and southernmost (Ben Lomond) munros, I have been on the most northerly island in the UK (Unst, Shetland) and taken the ferry to Stornoway from Ullapool as well seen snow on the beach in Aberdeen. I have stared in awe at the deepest loch in Scotland (1,000ft!), re-enacted Monty Python sketches at Doune and failed to learn to snowboard at Cairngorm Mountain. My love for Scotland runs deep, which is why I decided to start WeeQuiz, solely dedicated to quizzes about the beautiful country of Scotland.
