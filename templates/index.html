Did you know?

The Union of the Crowns in 1603 saw the accession of James VI of Scotland to to thrones of England & Ireland, uniting the three realms under a single monarch. A century later in 1707 The Union of Parliaments (combining the Union with Scotland Act 1706 & the Union with England Act 1707) saw the Kingdom of England & the Kingdom of Scotland become "united into One Kingdom by the name of Great Britain".
